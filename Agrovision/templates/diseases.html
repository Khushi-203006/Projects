1. Leaf Blight

Caused by fungal spores that attack leaves, leading to dark brown or black lesions. Prevent using crop rotation, proper spacing, and recommended fungicides.

2. Powdery Mildew

Identified by white powdery patches on leaf surfaces. Thrives in warm, dry climates. Improve plant spacing and ventilation to prevent infection.

3. Rust

Causes orange or brown powdery pustules on leaves. Common in humid and warm areas. Use resistant varieties and avoid overhead irrigation.
